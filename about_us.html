About Us

Our Story

Kappa Theta Pi is a distinguished co-ed professional Technology Fraternity, is dedicated to the promotion and advancement of technology-centric careers amongst collegiate students. Our fraternity is a nurturing hub for those who share a passion for technology, bridging the gap between academics and industry.




Our History

Kappa Theta Pi, a forward-thinking fraternity deeply rooted in technology, was founded in 2015 by a group of visionary students at the University of Colorado Boulder. Recognizing a gap in the university community, these students sought to create a unique fraternity experience that would transcend traditional social aspects, delving deep into the heart of the ever-evolving tech industry. The fraternity's founders dreamed of a space where like-minded individuals could explore their passion for technology, not merely as a hobby, but as a transformative tool for the future. They envisioned Kappa Theta Pi as a vibrant platform where members could engage in rich technological discourse, collaborate on innovative projects, and acquire vital skills for a future tech career.




DEI Commitment

At Kappa Theta Pi, we deeply value diversity, equity, and inclusion. Our strength lies in the unique qualities, perspectives, and experiences of our employees, regardless of age, color, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, national origin, race, religion, sexual orientation, or veteran status.
