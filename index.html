Pillars

Professional Development

KTP helps members build professional skills through interview training, resume building, one-on-one mentorship, private company recruiting, and more. We aim to prepare members for success in any technology-related career.

Alumni Connections

KTP has a vast alumni network, filled with people working all over the globe. From tech companies like Microsoft, Amazon, Facebook, Apple, and Google, to startups, consulting firms, and financial technology firms, this network has it all.

Social Growth

KTP is not just a professional fraternity, but also makes social connections a priority. We build and nuture these friendships through events including tailgates, bowling, retreats, and KTP formal.

Technical Advancement

We prioritize creating and expanding our members' technical skillsets. Whether it's by participating in one of our project teams or attending a workshop, there is always something to learn.

Academic Support

KTP Boulder is made up of some of the brightest minds across CU's campus. This vast network is there to provide help both inside and outside of the classroom.
